### Books
---
- Pearl et al. Causal inference in statistics: a primer. _John Wiley & Sons 2016._
- Peters et al. Elements of causal inference: foundations and learning algorithms. _MIT press 2017._
- Bishop et al. Pattern recognition and machine learning. _Springer 2006._
- Tengyu Ma. [Lecture notes for machine learning theory](https://github.com/tengyuma/cs229m_notes/tree/main).
- Mohri et al. Foundations of machine learning (second edition). _MIT press 2018._
- Pierre Alquier. [User-friendly introduction to PAC-Bayes bounds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.11216). _2024._
- Endre Suli and David F. Mayers. An introduction to numerical analysis. _Cambridge University press 2003._ 


### Papers
---
<b> Why importance weighting fails under overparameterization <b/>

- Zhai et al. [Understanding why generalized reweighting does not improve over ERM](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=ashPce_W8F-). _In ICLR 2023_.
- Xu et al. [Understanding the role of importance weighting for deep learning](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=_WnwtieRHxM). _In ICLR 2021_.
- Byrd and Lipton. [What is the effect of importance weighting in deep learning?](http://arxiv.org/abs/1812.03372) _In ICML 2019_.

<b> Long-tail learning <b/>
- Menon et al. [Long-tail learning via logit adjustment](http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.07314). _In ICLR 2021_.
- Ren et al. [Balanced MSE for imbalanced visual regression](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2022/papers/Ren_Balanced_MSE_for_Imbalanced_Visual_Regression_CVPR_2022_paper.pdf). _In CVPR 2022._
- Cao et al. [Learning imbalanced datasets with label-distribution-aware margin loss](https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2019/file/621461af90cadfdaf0e8d4cc25129f91-Paper.pdf). _In NeurIPS 2019._ 
- Kini et al. [Label-imbalanced and group-sensitive classification under overparameterization](https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper/2021/file/9dfcf16f0adbc5e2a55ef02db36bac7f-Paper.pdf). _In NeurIPS 2021._
- Kang et al. [Decoupling representation and classifier for long-tailed recognition](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=r1gRTCVFvB). _In ICLR 2020._

<b> Robust fine-tuning <b/>
- Wortsman et al. [Robust fine-tuning of zero-shot models](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2022/papers/Wortsman_Robust_Fine-Tuning_of_Zero-Shot_Models_CVPR_2022_paper.pdf). _In CVPR 2022_.
- Kumar et al. [Calibrated ensembles can mitigate accuracy tradeoffs under distribution shift](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v180/kumar22a/kumar22a.pdf). _In UAI 2022_. 
- Kumar et al. [Fine-tuning can distort pretrained features and underperform out-of-distribution](http://arxiv.org/abs/2202.10054). _In ICLR 2022_.
- Li et al. [Explicit inductive bias for transfer learning with convolutional networks](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v80/li18a/li18a.pdf). _In ICML 2018_.
- Tian et al. [Trainable projected gradient method for robust fine-tuning](https://doi.org/10.1109/CVPR52729.2023.00757). _In CVPR 2023_.

<b>Robustness<b/>

- Foret et al. [Sharpness-aware minimization for efficiently improving generalization](http://arxiv.org/abs/2010.01412). _In ICLR 2021_.
- Martin Arjovsky. [Out of distribution generalization in machine learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.02667). PhD Thesis.
- Qiu et al. [Simple and fast group robustness by automatic feature reweighting](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v202/qiu23c/qiu23c.pdf). _In ICML 2023_.
- Khani and Liang. [Removing spurious features can hurt accuracy and affect groups
disproportionately](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.04104). _In FAccT 2021_.
- Zhou et al. [Sparse invariant risk minimization](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v162/zhou22e.html). _In ICML 2022_.


<b>Ensemble<b/>
  
- Tumer and Ghosh. [Error correlation and error reduction in ensemble classifiers](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/095400996116839). _Connection science 1996_.
- Tumer and Ghosh. [Analysis of decision boundaries in linearly combined neural classifiers](https://doi.org/10.1016/0031-3203(95)00085-2). _PR 1996_.

<b>Others<b/>

- Sun et al. [Out-of-distribution detection with deep nearest neighbors](http://arxiv.org/abs/2204.06507). _In ICML 2022_.
- Du et al. [Algorithmic regularization in learning deep homogeneous models: layers are automatically balanced](http://arxiv.org/abs/1806.00900). _In NeurIPS 2018_.
- Bartlett et al. [Local rademacher complexities](https://doi.org/10.1214/009053605000000282). _The Annals of Statistics 2005_.
- Bartlett et al. [Benign overfitting in linear regression](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1907378117). _PNAS 2020_.
